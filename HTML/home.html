MENU RECOMMENDATIONS

NASI KUNING TUMPENG

Rice cooked with coconut milk combined with egg dishes, fried chicken, orek tempeh, balado potatoes, fried noodles, crackers and fresh vegetables.

BAKSO MALANG

Meatballs with various fillings such as cheese, chili, tendons, tofu, with fried meatballs, noodles, steamed dumplings, fried dumplings, and drizzled with savory broth.

SIOMAY BANDUNG

Processed fish with tapioca flour and combined with tofu, bitter melon, cabbage, boiled potatoes, boiled eggs, and served with peanut sauce and lime.

NASI BAKAR

Rice cooked with spices such as lemongrass, bay leaves, galangal, then filled with shredded chicken cooked using spices, chilies, basil and grilled using a banana leaf wrap.
