ABOUT US

CAteriNgz is your premier choice for providing a wide array of delicious and high-quality Indonesian specialties to cater to your diverse needs. Whether you are seeking packed lunches for the office, convenient lunch boxes for traveling, boxed rice for special events, or even the grand tumpeng rice to celebrate those truly special moments, CAteriNgz is here to meet all your culinary desires.

We take immense pride in serving exquisite dishes crafted from the finest ingredients that Indonesia has to offer. Our commitment to quality is evident in every bite, as each dish is meticulously prepared with love and care by our team of expert chefs. These culinary artisans are dedicated to bringing out the most authentic flavors, offering you an experience of Indonesian cuisine like never before.

At CAteriNgz, we understand that every event is unique, and so are your preferences. That's why we offer a variety of menu options and the flexibility to accommodate special requests, ensuring that each meal is tailored to your specific tastes. Whether you are in the bustling metropolis of Jakarta, the vibrant city of Bandung, or the charming town of Malang, we are committed to delivering an unforgettable culinary experience right to your doorstep.

Imagine the convenience and delight of having sumptuous Indonesian meals delivered to your location, ready to impress your guests and enhance your event. From corporate meetings to personal celebrations, our diverse offerings are designed to add a touch of elegance and flavor to any occasion. Our tumpeng rice, a traditional cone-shaped rice dish often enjoyed at festive gatherings, is just one example of the spectacular dishes we can provide, perfect for making your special moments even more memorable.

With CAteriNgz as your culinary partner, you can relax and focus on enjoying your event while we take care of the food. Our dedication to excellence and customer satisfaction ensures that every meal we deliver is of the highest standard, both in taste and presentation.

So, why wait? Let CAteriNgz elevate your next gathering with the best of Indonesian cuisine. Contact us today to explore our menu options and experience the unparalleled quality and service that only CAteriNgz can provide. Celebrate every important moment in your life with the delicious and authentic flavors of Indonesia, prepared and delivered with utmost care and precision by CAteriNgz.
